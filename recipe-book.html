Recipe #1 - Cheesecake

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired with a buttery graham cracker crust, no one can deny its simple decadence. For the best results, bake in a water bath.

  • Time: 7 hours, 45 minutes (includes chilling)
  • Serves 12-16 persons

Your Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

Ingredients Quantity
Graham cracker crumbs 1 and 1/2 cups (180g)
Granulated sugar 1/4 cup (50g)
Unsalted butter, melted 5 Tablespoons (71g)

Cheesecake Filling

Ingredients Quantity
Full-fat brick cream cheese, softened to room temperature 32 ounces (904g)
Granulated sugar 1 cup (200g)
Full-fat sour cream, at room temperature 1 cup (240g)
Pure vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended) 2 teaspoons
Large eggs, at room temperature 3

Instructions

- Adjust the oven rack to the lower-middle position and preheat oven to 350°F (177°C).

- Make the crust: If you’re starting out with full graham crackers, use a food processor or blender to grind them into fine crumbs. Pour into a medium bowl and stir in sugar until combined, and then stir in the melted butter. Mixture will be sandy.

- Make the filling: Using a handheld or stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar together on medium-high speed in a large bowl until the mixture is smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes.

- Add the sour cream, vanilla extract, and lemon juice then beat until fully combined. On medium speed, add the eggs one at a time, beating after each addition until just blended. After the final egg is incorporated into the batter, stop mixing.

- Boil a pot of water. You need 1 inch of water in your roasting pan for the water bath, so make sure you boil enough. I use an entire kettle of hot water. As the water is heating up, wrap the aluminum foil around the springform pan.

- Pour the cheesecake batter on top of the crust. Use a rubber spatula or spoon to smooth it into an even layer.

- Bake cheesecake for 55–70 minutes or until the center is almost set

- Use a knife to loosen the chilled cheesecake from the rim of the springform pan, then remove the rim. Using a clean sharp knife, cut into slices for serving. For neat slices, wipe the knife clean and dip into warm water between each slice.

Recipe #2 - Burger

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves 4 persons

Your Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Lean ground beef 1 ½ pounds
Finely chopped ½ onion
Shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese ½ cup
Egg 1
Envelope dry onion soup mix 1 ounce
Garlic, minced 1 clove
Soy sauce 1 teaspoon
Worcestershire sauce 1 teaspoon
Dried parsley 1 teaspoon
Dried basil 1 teaspoon
Dried oregano 1 teaspoon
Crushed dried rosemary ½ teaspoon
Salt and Pepper However much needed

Instructions

- Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

- Meanwhile, combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.

- Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

Source: allrecipes.com


Recipe #3 - Fettuccine Alfredo

There are certain recipes that are essential to have in your recipe box and one of them has to be a phenomenal Fettuccine alfredo recipe.

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves 5 persons

Your Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Fettuccine Pasta 1 lb
Garlic Clove (minced) 1
Heavy Cream 1 & 1/2 cups
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Shredded Parmesan Cheese 1 & 1/4 cup
Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Italian Parsley (optional) 2 Tablespoons
Butter 6 Tablespoons

Instructions

- In a large pot, heat water over high heat until boiling. Add salt to season the water. Once it is boiling, add fettuccine and cook according to package instructions.

- In a large skillet or pan, heat butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in heavy cream.

- Let heavy cream reduce and cook for 5 to 8 minutes. Add half of the parmesan cheese to the mixture and whisk well until smooth. Keep over heat and whisk well until cheese is melted.

- Save some pasta water. The pasta water is full of flavor and can be used to thin out the sauce.

- Toss alfredo sauce with fettuccine pasta and add half of the parmesan cheese. Once it is tossed, garnish with the remaining parmesan cheese. Add a little pasta water if it needs to be thinned out.

- Garnish with Italian parsley, if so desired.

Source: modernhoney.com
